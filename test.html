Write a Message

“I can shake off everything as I write; my sorrows disappear, my courage is reborn.”
— Anne Frank

    - Write an original message for Anne Frank on the leaf provided.
    - Write atleast 3 sentences using all lines (no fluff or repetition).
    - Consider the lessons you learned from Anne, her ideals and values, why she in inspirational, EGPs, etc. Be respectful and sincere!
    - Do NOT summarize the play or Anne's experience.
    - Make sure to flip the leaf and sign your name! Click “Send” once done.
